# Waiver and safety policy (Front Range Climbing - Boulder)

All climbers and visitors must have a signed waiver on file.

- Minors must have a waiver signed by a parent/legal guardian.
- Belay requires a current belay check and gym belay approval.
- Auto-belays: inspect harness and attachment before each climb.
- No loose chalk outside chalk bags; no liquid chalk in youth programs.

If you're unsure about a requirement, ask a staff member at the front desk.
