# Access, memberships, and coaching (overview)

## Facility access

- **Day Pass**: single-day facility access.
- **5-Pack Day Passes**: discounted bundle of five visits.
- **All-Access Monthly Membership**: facility access plus **lockers** and **usage of shared equipment**.

## Private coaching

Private coaching is a separate purchase from facility access.

- **Private Coaching (60 minutes)**: 1:1 instruction session
- **Requires facility access** (a day pass or membership) to enter the gym for the session.
