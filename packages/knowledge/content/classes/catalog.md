# Class catalog (high level)

## Group classes

- **Bouldering Fundamentals (Beginner)**: movement basics, falling safely, gym etiquette.
- **Top Rope Basics (Beginner)**: harness fit, knot, belay technique, partner checks.
- **Lead Climbing + Belay (Intermediate)**: clipping, lead belay, lead falls, gym lead test prep.
- **Outdoor Wall Intro + Safety (Beginner)**: outdoor wall orientation and safety checks. Weather-dependent.
- **Outdoor Lead Skills Clinic (Intermediate)**: outdoor lead skills practice. Weather-dependent.

## Private coaching

Private sessions are 1:1 with a coach and can focus on:

- Movement coaching (bouldering or ropes)
- Lead progression
- Technique clinics (heel hooks, body tension, footwork)

If you want a specific coach, ask and we’ll try to match availability.
